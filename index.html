Welcome to The Dream Factory

What's a dream factory?

A dream factory is where everything comes together to bring dreams to fruition. That's what the School for Advanced Studies is.

The School for Advanced Studies (SAS) is a nationally recognized collegiate high school of excellence, the combined effort of Miami-Dade County Public Schools (MDCPS) and Miami-Dade College (MDC) . Students attending SAS complete their last two years of high school while attending MDC. This opportunity for acceleration and saturated enrichment attracts highly motivated and academically talented students. SAS prides itself on its family-like environment, small school setting, and focus on a quality education. School for Advanced Studies provides a uniquely supportive transition between secondary and post-secondary education.
