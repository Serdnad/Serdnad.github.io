Welcome to The Dream Factory

What's a dream factory?

A dream factory is a factory that brings dreams into fruition. That's exactly what the School for Advanced Studies does.

The School for Advanced Studies (SAS) is a nationally recognized collegiate high school of excellence, a combined effort of Miami-Dade County Public Schools (MDCPS) and Miami-Dade College (MDC) . Students attending SAS complete their last two years of high school while attending MDC. The opportunity for acceleration and enrichment attracts motivated and academically talented students. SAS takes pride in its family-like environment, small school setting, and focus on a quality education. School for Advanced Studies provides a uniquely supportive transition between secondary and post-secondary education.
